Top down shooter about a triangle called Pitagunas.

### Controls

* 1, 2, 3 To switch weapons.
* Shit / Q to do a dash.
* W to accelerate.
* Click to shoot

### Info

* Objective: Collect pentagons.
* Motivations: none of them.
* Engine: Unity 2019.4.11
